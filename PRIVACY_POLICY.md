# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-26

Heimdall Family Protection is designed to protect families from scams, coercion, and risky digital behavior while minimizing personal data collection.

## What Heimdall Does

Heimdall helps parents and guardians identify high-risk situations such as:

- pressure to keep secrets;
- requests for SMS codes or passwords;
- money transfers or bank actions;
- remote-access app installation;
- requests for address, photos, documents, or location;
- movement from games or public chats into private messengers.

## What Heimdall Does Not Do

Heimdall does not silently read private messenger conversations.

Heimdall does not collect full chat histories.

Heimdall does not sell family data.

Heimdall does not use hidden surveillance as a product model.

## Data Processed

Depending on the product version and user consent, Heimdall may process:

- family account information;
- device identifiers needed for protection;
- parent-approved protection settings;
- risk events and alert metadata;
- text fragments explicitly shared to Heimdall for analysis;
- Screen Time API events available through Apple Family Controls, Device Activity, and Managed Settings.

## Data Minimization

Parents should receive risk reasons and recommended actions, not complete private conversations. Heimdall is built to show why something is dangerous while avoiding unnecessary exposure of personal content.

## Children and Family Safety

Heimdall is intended to be configured by a parent or guardian. Family members should be told what the protection does and why it is used.

## Security

Production versions should use encryption in transit, limited data retention, access controls, and audit logging for family safety events.
