Production layer

What is needed for real protection, not a mockup

The site is now the parent console. To actually protect iPhones it needs the next layer: a Heimdall iOS app with Apple's Family Controls entitlement, a policy server and push notifications. Without that, no website can secretly read Telegram or control apps on iPhone.
