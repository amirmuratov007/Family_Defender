Setup

What the parent does

1

Open the site on the parent phone

The parent opens the family console and creates a family profile. This is the web version; the next production step is a native iOS client.

2

Install Heimdall on the child iPhone

Through App Store or TestFlight. The child does not configure protection alone: the parent enables and approves the device.

3

Enable iOS family controls

Real app shielding and device activity require Apple's Family Controls entitlement. The Apple request document is already in the project.

4

Receive alerts

Alerts are raised for new contacts, migration from games to messengers, requests for codes, money or secrecy.

How it works

The honest iPhone architecture

A website cannot silently read Telegram on iPhone. Apple sandboxing and encrypted messengers do not allow a normal site or app to invisibly inspect private chats.

The real product is a parent web console, a native iOS app on the child device, Apple Family Controls entitlement, an alert server and push notifications.

Before entitlement approval, the web console works as the product cockpit and TestFlight can validate the interface. After approval, app shielding and event-based protection become possible.
